About Me

I am a passionate and driven designer and developer with 5 years of experience working with game engines such as Unity (2 years) and Unreal Engine (5 years). I am skilled with Unreal Engines blueprint scripting, C++, C#, blockmeshing, animation, sound design, debugging, troubleshooting, lighting, packaging and deploying, as well as publishing to platforms on Steam, Xbox Store and Nintendo eShop. I also have knowledge and skills in IT, Networks, Cybersecurity and Web Development.

I went to Fresno City College for Computer Information Systems, as well as Full Sail University for Game Design.

I have also built my own homelab, including a server running an Active Directory for my home, Proxmox as a Hypervisor, with LXC containers for Docker, Portainer, TrueNAS as well as virtual machines for Linux and Windows Server and a private Terraria server. I have also set up a VLAN in my home to isolate my wireless devices, as well as my IoT devices.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking, reading, fixing computers and other electronic devices, as well as spending time with my cat, Azshara.
